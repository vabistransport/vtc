More than just transport

Vabis Transport is a united team of drivers who value discipline, mutual respect and realism. We strive to create the best conditions for cooperative gameplay and the development of every member. Our company was founded by Nikita Ariphe on July 30, 2025, and since then we have grown into a friendly and active community.

  • Automatic trip tracking via our custom Vabis Hub platform using the smart Vabis Tracker client, or via Trucky. Every member must be active in at least one of these tracking systems.
  • Regular convoys and events Chill Vabis convoys where the main rule is stylish driving! We also actively participate in various public convoys hosted by other transport companies.
  • Ranking and progress system Climb the career ladder from a beginner to the top. Your rank is based on the total mileage successfully recorded via Vabis Hub or Trucky.
  • Friendly community A supportive team, our own unique VTC paint job, advanced IT infrastructure (private Vabis Hub and custom Discord bot), and a monthly "Driver of the Month" award for the most dedicated members.
